Mission and Values

Mission is a concise and objective statement of purpose of an organization, explaining its purpose of existence and the reasons for its creation. From this perspective, the UNFAM's mission is the following: production, dissemination and application of the scientific and technological knowledge in order to participate fully in the formation of citizens and make them more complete, aiming at their full integration into society, politics, culture and ethics. With the aim of social transformation and regional development, has become a reference in teaching, research, promotion and innovation. In its activities, UNFAM will value the moral commitment to social responsibility, respect, transparency, excellence and determination, in in line with the basic principles of citizenship and humanitarianism, freedom of expression, a sense of unity and culture.
